FAQs about SAP Business One Pricing

Professional License: Offers full access to all features and functionalities of SAP Business One. Ideal for users who require comprehensive tools and capabilities.

Limited License: Provides access to a subset of functionalities, suitable for users who need to perform specific tasks like financial management or sales orders.

The annual maintenance fee is typically a percentage of the software's total cost, providing updates, support, and new features.

The implementation process involves initial analysis, system design, data migration, user training, and final deployment. It can vary in duration, generally taking several weeks to months, depending on business size and complexity.

Flexible payment options may include monthly or quarterly plans, financing, or customized payment schedules to accommodate small business budgets.
